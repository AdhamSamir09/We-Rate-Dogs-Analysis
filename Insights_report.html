Project: Analyze Report¶

Analyzing and Visualizing Data¶

By using the data we wrangled previously in the wrangle_act.ipynb , there are many insights that can be extracted. Here are a few ones I found interesting with their visualization.

Insights from our wrangled data:¶

  1. The best month for tweets is number 6 or "June" as it has the highest average in both retweets_count & favorited_count from all the months across 3 years.

  2. The best category of dogs to tweet are puppies or "puppo" as they get the highest average in both retweet_count & favorite_count.

  3. The breed with the highest average rating percentage is the "Clumber" breed.

Insight #1: The best month to make tweets¶

Here I grouped the data by month to figure out which month has the highest average retweets and favorites across the 3 years.
We found that month 6 or "June" had the top scores in both.

To check which dog breed did the weRateDogs page give the highest average rating to, I wrote the following code
I found that a breed called "Clumber" had the highest with a staggering 270% !!

master_dataset["probable_breed"] = master_dataset["category"]
master_dataset["rating_percentage"] = master_dataset["rating_numerator"] / master_dataset["rating_denominator"] * 100  
for index, row in master_dataset.iterrows():
    if row["p1_dog"] == True:
        master_dataset.loc[index, "probable_breed"] = row["p1"]
    elif row["p2_dog"] == True:
        master_dataset.loc[index, "probable_breed"] = row["p2"]
    elif row["p3_dog"] == True:
        master_dataset.loc[index, "probable_breed"] = row["p3"]
    else:
        continue
